KEY FEATURES

AI
Integration

Conmune is integrated with an Artificial Intelligence that is able to identify keywords in messages and respond with relevant messages. Our AI is trained in the Conmune system to deliver messages based on both general and specific situations ensuring that your clients receive a seamless experience in conversating with your business.

Easy
Adaption

Conmune is a system that can be used on various chat platforms. At the moment, Conmune is allowing businesses to interact with their clients through WhatsApp.

ChatBot

Conmune has an integrated chatbot system that will handle all communications on behalf of the businesses. Combined with the AI feature, the chatbot will be able to interact with your clients in a seamless manner to resolve all queries and requests.
